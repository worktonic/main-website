About Drawing Board

WorkTonic is a subsidiary of Drawing Board Advisory & Consulting Services.

Work Tonic is a division of Drawing Board Advisory & Consulting Services – a boutique management consulting company for SME. Drawing Board founded in 2016, has been assisting companies which are at the intersection of scaling up and growing professionally, endeavouring to drive operational excellence and grow their business profitably.

Drawing Board has assisted leading companies across Real Estate, Hospitality, Healthcare, Cinema, Kids Prep School, Consumer Durable, Automobile to name a few.

The founder, K. Subramanian comes with close to 3 decades of experience across FMCG, Automobile Retail and Consulting. In his last formal role, he was the CEO of Jubilant MotorWorks (a division of Jubilant Bhartia Group) between 2008 and 2016.
