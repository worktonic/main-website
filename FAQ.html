Work Tonic is open for all unemployed graduates and post graduates only. It doesn't have a program for undergraduates or dropouts.

The fastest growth in career comes to those who excel in their Sales and Marketing roles. Most companies appoint CEO with proven track record in Sales and Marketing. Sales careers are enjoyable as they offer excellent job satisfaction, promotions and career growth, financial growth, performance based rewards and recognition. After 4-7 years in Sales, many Sales professionals are entrusted to manage teams, geographies, sales margins, brand promotion and executions etc.


Good Sales professionals who have a performance track record are always in demand and they go onto become managers first. The high potential managers are later groomed and transformed into leaders by the organizations.

The following skill sets are useful to succeed in a Sales career. Kindly ask yourself if you have some of these as your skills in some degree? However, it is not expected that one needs to have all the skills. Many start with few basic inherent skills and go on to build the other skills that make them proficient in selling.


If you possess some skills naturally and if our counsellors concurs, then you are destined to becoming a Top Sales professional.


The characteristics of a good Sales professional are

  • They are confident and optimistic
  • They are good listeners and communicate well
  • They are assertive, friendly and build relationships
  • They are expressive and persuasive.
  • They are extroverted
  • They are resilient and don't give up easily
  • They are multi-taskers
  • They are focused and keep achieving their smaller goals
  • They are updated on current affairs and have a broad worldview.
  • They are honest and committed.

Even if you think that the above skills are not in you - our counsellors will assess your readiness for any of the courses we offer and suggest you. You make the decision and feel right about it.

All students desiring to enroll for this program are requested to visit our Centre and meet the counsellors. They will do a preliminary discussion followed by a short assessment in Basic Math and English which may take a max of 45 minutes. The assessment is designed to understand the student’s aptitude, attitude and readiness revealing the student’s suitability and the right course. It is advised that the students opt for the course that is thrown by the assessments. However, there’s no coercion. Once the enrollment is completed, the students can join the course on the commencement date and attend for the duration of the course.

The General Assessments are designed to measure candidates’ basic numerical ability, communication skills, general awareness, reasoning, critical thinking, aspirations, learning interest, personality type and cognitive skills.


The tests are designed to test the candidate’s latent ability to learn new skill or acquire knowledge, application and their tenacity to challenges.

General Assessments is designed to only test the basic skills. Passing the General Assessments is mandatory for enrolment into any of Work Tonic’s courses. Exceptional students are offered 10% discount in the course fee.

Sales is the oxygen for any company and sales skills are vital for all business ventures. All companies are measured by the external bodies (including investors) basis their performance in sales growth, revenue growth and profitability. All organizations succeed by building a strong sales force and distribution network that ensures the product availability across the geography of the market.


In any given industry, when companies come with deep pockets to build products, brands and invest in capacities – the differentiating force is always the Quality of Salesforce. Companies always look for talent to build strong Sales force to meet their organic growth requirements and to retain their leadership.


The job market is always abuzz with vacancies in Sales & Marketing as companies keep expanding their geographies and scale up their business with new products and extensions. Our Founder has spent his career spanning 25 years selling Garments, Beverages, Luxury Auto Retail and has a wider exposure to selling in Real Estate / Hospitality / Durables / Cinema Advertising. Hence the programs have the Sales and Customer Care theme in the core offering.

Work Tonic training program 'Sales and Business Development' is structured to equip the students with Sales fundamentals that are applicable to most industries such as FMCG / Retail / Auto / Electronics, Durables and Appliances / Real Estate / Paint & Cement / Healthcare / Hospitality and Building Construction Material. Once the base knowledge of Selling is taught, the course dwells deeper into each of the above industry to offer richer industry specific insights and their nuances. It is a good opportunity for a student to develop interest in any of these industries and explore it for their first job.


The training program 'Customer Care and Delight' is structured to familiarize the students with the basics of selling and gradually built to in depth understanding of what contributes to customer satisfaction, and customer delight. Here students are trained to be empathetic, carry the right attitude, anticipate customer needs, be consistent, be solution oriented, how to build professional relationships with their customers, Customer happiness measurement tools etc. The program is then extrapolated to appreciate what constitutes to customer delight across various industries.


All the above programs are for 30 days averaging 4 hours of classroom each day and 4 hours of field / research work.
