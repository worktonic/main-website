Course Description

Get trained from the best.

  • Work Tonic training programs are structured to equip the students with fundamentals that are applicable to most industries such as FMCG, Retail, Auto, Electronics and Appliances, Real Estate, Building Construction, Healthcare, Education and Hospitality.
  • Further, the course dwells deeper for richer industry specific insights and their nuances.
  • Through the Soft skill and Leadership skill training, students are trained to be apply the right skills, have a winning attitude, be solution oriented, take responsibilities, have a curious and a learning mindset, build professional relationships with their customers and much more.
  • The program is then extrapolated to appreciate what constitutes to customer delight across various industries.
  • The training content has been ably developed under the watchful supervision of its Founder – K. Subramanian who has 25 years of rich and diversified exposure across FMCG, Auto Retail and Management Consulting.
  • The training content is enriched with inputs from diverse industry leaders who also serve as Guest Faculty at Work Tonic.

Batches start every first working day of the month.
